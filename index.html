MARELD

Originating from the coastal town of Varberg on the Swedish West Coast, the progressive metal outfit MARELD started taking form in early 2019 and has been in the works ever since. With each member having a different musical background, Mareld comes together with one common goal; to create fun music.

Technical guitar riffs, heavy breakdowns, groovy bass parts, classic rock vocals and brutal death metal screams all blend together in perfect harmony. MARELD tries to embrace the DIY-mentality in as many ways as possible. Everything you hear is self produced.
